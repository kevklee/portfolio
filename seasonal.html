About

Seasonal is a Los Angeles Recyclathon Hackathon project created by multiple members as a team. This website solves the problem of LA opensource data making it available to the public.

Problem

The difficult problem in terms of the user experience was how best to make opensource city data be most accessible to the user. Another problem was to think of design that was acceptable by the city as well as the residents of Los Angeles.

Solution

The design solution had to be very simple. This is because the city residents as well as the city does not have the resources to sift through any sort of complexity. The design by default is make so that any data from a specific time or location is made available on the website with the use of graphs and maps.

App

The app was design on the same principle of simplicity with usability in mind. The our hackathon group decided that the public would be more interested if the city data could be utilized or be helpful to a resident's day to day tasks. We decided that the city app would be a grocery list app that could track recyclables.
